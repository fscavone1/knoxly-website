Before we start...

First, you need to take a preliminary test to verify your basic knowledge about privacy principles nowadays, that is if you can correctly identify sensitive contents into twitter messages. In the table on the right, you can find the rules you have to follow both for the preliminary and the official tests. As you can see, concepts considered sensitive are marked with ✓, viceversa with ×: for example, data relating to objects are considered not sensitive; otherwise, data relating to job are considered sensitive.
We consider the following topics: politics, health, job, travel, racism, religion and sexual orientation.
In the preliminary test, you will have to answer a question for each topic and you will pass it only if you give us at least 5 correct answers. In the official test, you can answer as many questions as you want, you can quit anytime.

Thank you for taking part to this project! We hope you enjoy the test.
